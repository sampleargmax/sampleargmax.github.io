Research Interests

LLM Post-Training Reinforcement Learning (RLVR) Agent & Tool Use Reasoning Instruction Following Deep Reinforcement Learning Cloud Resource Scheduling

During my PhD I focused on applying deep reinforcement learning to cloud resource scheduling, modeling decision problems via neural-network generalization to achieve better solution quality and faster response times — covering MDP modeling, Uplift modeling, and offline/online RL. Since joining industry, I have shifted towards LLM post-training: studying RLVR algorithms and their applications to agentic tasks, tool calling, multi-step reasoning, and instruction following.
